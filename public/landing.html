FAQ

Common questions

Is RUBAROO really free? ▼
Yes! Creating a profile, browsing matches, and chatting is completely free. Plus and Gold give you more features but you can find your person on the free plan.
How does verification work? ▼
Every user verifies their phone number via OTP. You can optionally verify with Aadhaar for a trust badge. Our team reviews all profiles within 24 hours.
Is my data safe? ▼
Absolutely. We're fully compliant with India's DPDP Act 2023. Your data is encrypted and never sold to third parties. You can delete your account anytime.
How does the AI matching work? ▼
Our AI analyzes 47 personality dimensions including communication style, values, lifestyle, and intent — not just photos. The more you use RUBAROO, the smarter it gets.
Is RUBAROO safe for women? ▼
Women's safety is our top priority. Women message first, your number is always hidden, 1-tap report and block, 24/7 safety team. We take all reports seriously.
Which cities are available? ▼
RUBAROO is available across 50+ cities in India including all metros and major Tier 2 cities. We're adding new cities every month.
